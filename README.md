# Contract Management System

## Overview
The Contract Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the entire lifecycle of enterprise contracts. From initial requisition and vendor selection to active management and archival, this system provides a centralized hub for legal, procurement, and management teams.

## Key Features

### 1. Unified Dashboard
A command center that provides instant visibility into the organization's contract landscape.
- **Actionable Insights**: Highlight contracts requiring immediate attention (e.g., expiring soon, pending approval).
- **Recent Activity**: detailed audit trail of all contract updates and workflow changes.
- **Quick Stats**: Real-time metrics on total utilization and active engagements.

### 2. Contract Repository
A structured, searchable database for all contract records.
- **Categorized Views**: Filter contracts by status (Ongoing, Active, Expiring, Finished).
- **Smart Search**: Quickly locate contracts by vendor, division, or contract number.
- **Version Control**: Access current and historical versions of every agreement.

### 3. Bid Agenda & Workflow
An integrated workspace for managing the complex vendor selection process.
- **Step-by-Step Workflow**: Structured stages for KYC (Know Your Customer), Technical Evaluation, and Commercial Negotiation.
- **Vendor Comparison**: Side-by-side analysis of vendor capabilities, compliance, and pricing.
- **Collaborative Scoring**: Team-based input for technical and commercial assessments.

### 4. Performance Analytics
Data-driven tools to optimize procurement and legal operations.
- **Process Efficiency**: Tracking turnaround times (Lead Time) for contract creation.
- **Team Performance**: Workload distribution and completion rates by PIC (Person In Charge).
- **Cost Savings**: Analysis of negotiated savings vs. initial quotes.

## Business Benefits
- **Risk Mitigation**: Proactive alerts key dates prevent unintentional renewals or lapses in coverage.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Standardized workflows reduce manual overhead and "email tag."
- **Transparency**: Clear audit trails ensure accountability and compliance at every stage.
